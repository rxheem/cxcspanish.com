    <section>
      <p>
        Directed Situations are short scenarios or situations in
        which you are required to provide a one sentence response
        in Spanish.
      </p>

      <p>Example, you are given the following scenario:</p>

      <div class="notification is-info">
        <p>
          "Your friend’s birthday is coming up. Write the text
          message you send him with well wishes."
        </p>
      </div>

      <p>In this example, you are required to:</p>

      <ul>
        <li>
          Acknowledge that it’s your friend’s birthday, and,
        </li>
        <li>Wish him well wishes.</li>
      </ul>

      <h6 class="title" style="padding-top: 10px;">
        Suggested Responses
      </h6>

      <div>
        <input
          type="checkbox"
          id="example1"
          name="q"
          class="questions"
        />
        <div class="plus">
          <i
            style="color: coral;"
            class="fas fa-angle-double-right"
          ></i>
        </div>
        <label for="example1" class="question">
          <a href="#"> Feliz cumpleaños</a> Juan y yo espero que
          <a href="../tenses/subjunctive-mood.html">tú tengas</a>
          un buen día.
        </label>

        <div class="answers">
          <p>
            Happy birthday, Juan, and I hope you have a great day.
          </p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div>
        <input
          type="checkbox"
          id="example2"
          name="q"
          class="questions"
        />
        <div class="plus">
          <i
            style="color: coral;"
            class="fas fa-angle-double-right"
          ></i>
        </div>
        <label for="example2" class="question">
          ¡Feliz cumpleaños!</label
        >

        <div class="answers">
          <p>
            Happy birthday!
          </p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="alert alert-danger" role="alert">
        <span
          ><i
            style="color: red;"
            class="fas fa-radiation nav-icon"
          ></i>
          <b>Important</b>
        </span>
        <p style="padding-top: 5px!important;">
          You may be penalised if you write more than one
          sentences.
        </p>
      </div>

      <p>Let's take a look at another example:</p>

      <div class="notification is-info">
        <p>
          "You wish to make a request to your teacher. Write a
          note making the request."
        </p>
      </div>

      <p>In this example, you are required to:</p>

      <ul>
        <li>Make a request to your teacher</li>
      </ul>

      <div>
        <input
          type="checkbox"
          id="example3"
          name="q"
          class="questions"
        />
        <div class="plus">
          <i
            style="color: coral;"
            class="fas fa-angle-double-right"
          ></i>
        </div>
        <label for="example3" class="question">
          ¿Puedo ir al baño, por favor?</label
        >

        <div class="answers">
          <p>
            May I go to the bathroom, please?
          </p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div>
        <input
          type="checkbox"
          id="example4"
          name="q"
          class="questions"
        />
        <div class="plus">
          <i
            style="color: coral;"
            class="fas fa-angle-double-right"
          ></i>
        </div>
        <label for="example4" class="question">
          ¿Puede repetir, por favor?</label
        >

        <div class="answers">
          <p>
            Can you repeat, please?
          </p>

          <div class="alert alert-primary" role="alert">
            Remeber that you must use the polite form (usted or
            ustedes) when speaking with adults or someone with
            authority.
          </div>
        </div>
