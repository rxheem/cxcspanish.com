Directed Situations are short scenarios or situations in which you are required to provide a one sentence response in Spanish.

Example, you are given the following scenario:

"Your friendâs birthday is coming up. Write the text message you send him with well wishes."

  • Wish him well wishes.
Suggested Responses

Happy birthday!

Important

You may be penalised if you write more than one sentences.

Let's take a look at another example:

"You wish to make a request to your teacher. Write a note making the request."

In this example, you are required to:

  • Make a request to your teacher

May I go to the bathroom, please?

Can you repeat, please?

Remeber that you must use the polite form (usted or ustedes) when speaking with adults or someone with authority.
